The StoryBrand Advantage

Why the Right Words Matter More Than the Right Colours

Most websites focus on looking good. Yours already does that. The missing piece is what the website says and how it makes people feel.

The StoryBrand framework, developed by Donald Miller, is used by over 10,000 businesses worldwide. It's based on a simple insight: customers don't buy the best product — they buy the one they understand the fastest.

Right now, your site tells visitors what you do. After our rebuild, it will answer the three questions every visitor asks within five seconds:

1. What do you offer?

Immediately clear — no guessing, no jargon.

2. How will it improve my life?

An aspirational picture of life with your furniture.

3. What do I do next?

A clear, confident call to action — every time.

We'll also add testimonials, a clear step-by-step process, and a section that paints a picture of what life looks like after working with Luke Jones Furniture. These aren't nice-to-haves — they're the building blocks of trust.
