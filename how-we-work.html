Our Process

How We Work

From first conversation to final installation, we guide you through every step of creating your bespoke furniture.

A Leap of Faith Made Simple

Commissioning bespoke furniture is a significant decision. You're trusting us to understand your vision and bring it to life. We take that trust seriously. Our process is designed to give you confidence at every stage — with clear communication, detailed planning, and the opportunity to see your furniture taking shape.

Step One

Your Vision

It starts with a conversation. We'll arrange an informal visit to your home to discuss your ideas, see your space, and understand how you live. Bring your inspiration — Pinterest boards, magazine clippings, photos of furniture you love. There's no wrong answer and no commitment at this stage.

We'll talk about dimensions, colour schemes, materials you're drawn to, and how the furniture needs to function in your daily life. This is about understanding you as much as understanding your space.

What to have ready:

  • Approximate room dimensions
  • Inspiration images or mood boards
  • Colour and material preferences
  • Any specific storage or functionality needs
Step Two

Design & Planning

This is where your ideas take shape. Our designers create comprehensive CAD drawings that show exactly what your furniture will look like — cabinet configurations, drawer layouts, material specifications, and how everything fits together.

We'll visit your home at least twice during this phase: once to take precise measurements, and again to present and refine the designs. You'll see every detail — from the position of electrical sockets to the exact grain of the wood — before we make a single cut.

Nothing is left to chance. By the time we begin manufacturing, you'll know exactly what you're getting.

Step Three

Crafted by Hand

Your furniture is built in our workshop at Greinan Farm, Chipperfield. Over 6-12 weeks, skilled craftsmen bring the designs to life — selecting materials, cutting joints, assembling cabinets, and applying finishes by hand.

This isn't factory production. Every piece receives individual attention from start to finish. And unlike most furniture makers, we invite you to visit the workshop by appointment. Watch your kitchen, bedroom or study taking shape. Meet the people making it. See the craftsmanship firsthand.

There's something special about watching your furniture being built. It makes the final result even more meaningful.

Step Four

Installed & Guaranteed

Our installation team treats your home with the same care we put into making your furniture. We protect floors and surfaces, work cleanly, and pay meticulous attention to every detail — from perfectly aligned doors to seamlessly fitted panels.

Once installed, we walk through everything with you, explaining how to care for your new furniture and answering any questions. Your furniture comes with our quality guarantee, and we're always here for ongoing support and aftercare.

This isn't a transaction that ends at installation. We build relationships that last.

Our Workshop

Greinan Farm, Chipperfield

Our workshop sits in the Hertfordshire countryside, a few miles from Watford, Rickmansworth and Amersham. It's a working space — not a showroom — where real furniture is made by real craftsmen.

Visit by appointment to see current projects in progress, discuss materials, or simply get a feel for how we work. We're proud of what we do and we love showing people around.

Opening hours: Monday–Friday, 9am–5pm. Saturdays by appointment only.
