Our Process

From Vision to Reality

Commissioning bespoke furniture is a leap of faith. We make that journey simple, transparent and enjoyable.

01

Your Vision

Share your inspiration and ideas. We'll arrange an informal visit to discuss your project and understand exactly what you're looking for.

02

Design & Planning

Our designers create full CAD drawings covering layouts, materials and technical specifications. Every detail is planned before we begin.

03

Crafted by Hand

Your furniture is built in our Chipperfield workshop over 6-12 weeks. Visit by appointment to watch it take shape.

04

Installed & Guaranteed

Professional installation with meticulous attention to detail, backed by our quality guarantee and ongoing aftercare.
