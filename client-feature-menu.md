# Website Feature Menu

A guide to available features, tiers, and their benefits. Choose the options that best fit your goals and budget.

---

## 1. Search Engine Optimisation (SEO)

### On-Page SEO — Basic
- Unique meta titles and descriptions for every page
- Canonical links to prevent duplicate content issues
- Descriptive alt text on all images
- Clean heading hierarchy (H1–H6)
- SEO-friendly URL structures
- XML sitemap and robots.txt

**Benefit:** Your site becomes discoverable by search engines and starts appearing in relevant results.

### On-Page SEO — Advanced
- Everything in Basic, plus:
- Structured data / schema markup (Organisation, LocalBusiness, Product, FAQ, BreadcrumbList)
- Open Graph and Twitter Card meta tags for rich social sharing
- Internal linking strategy
- Keyword-optimised page copy (working from your target keywords)
- Core Web Vitals optimisation (performance, layout shift, interactivity)
- Image optimisation (modern formats like WebP/AVIF, lazy loading, responsive sizes)
- 301 redirect mapping from any existing site

**Benefit:** Stronger ranking signals, rich results in Google (star ratings, FAQs, breadcrumbs), faster load times, and a better experience that Google rewards.

### Ongoing SEO Retainer
- Monthly performance reporting (Google Search Console + Analytics)
- Content recommendations based on search trends
- Technical SEO health checks
- Competitor monitoring
- New page/blog post optimisation

**Benefit:** SEO is not a one-off task. Ongoing attention keeps you climbing the rankings and responding to algorithm changes.

## 2. Contact & Enquiry Forms

### Basic — Email Forwarding
- Simple contact form (name, email, message)
- Submissions sent directly to your email inbox
- Basic spam protection (honeypot + rate limiting)
- Confirmation message shown on submission

**Benefit:** Quick and low-cost. You receive enquiries straight to your inbox with no extra tools to learn.

### Standard — Database + Email Notifications
- Custom form fields tailored to your business (e.g. project type, budget range, timeline)
- Submissions stored in a secure database with an admin dashboard
- Email notification on each submission
- Ability to search, filter, and export enquiries
- File/image upload support
- Advanced spam protection (reCAPTCHA or Turnstile)

**Benefit:** You never lose an enquiry. Searchable history, better spam filtering, and structured data means you can follow up more effectively.

### Professional — CRM Integration
- Everything in Standard, plus:
- Integration with a CRM (e.g. HubSpot, Pipedrive, Salesforce, or a bespoke lightweight CRM)
- Automated lead status tracking (new, contacted, quoted, won, lost)
- Automated follow-up email sequences
- Pipeline/funnel reporting
- Team assignment and notes on each lead

**Benefit:** Turns your website into a proper sales tool. No leads slip through the cracks, follow-ups are automated, and you can see your pipeline at a glance.

## 3. Analytics & Tracking

### Basic — Privacy-Friendly Analytics
- Cookie-free analytics (e.g. Plausible, Umami, or Fathom)
- Page views, referrers, top pages, device/browser breakdown
- No cookie banner required
- GDPR/PECR compliant out of the box

**Benefit:** Understand your traffic without annoying cookie popups or privacy concerns.

### Advanced — Full Marketing Analytics
- Google Analytics 4 setup with custom events
- Google Tag Manager configuration
- Conversion tracking (form submissions, phone clicks, downloads)
- Cookie consent management (compliant banner)
- Goal and funnel reporting
- Integration with Google Ads or Meta Pixel if running paid campaigns

**Benefit:** Detailed insight into what's working, what's converting, and where to focus your marketing spend.

---

## 4. Legal & Compliance

### Basic — Essential Pages
- Privacy policy page (templated, tailored to your data processing)
- Cookie policy (if applicable)
- Terms and conditions page

**Benefit:** Meets minimum legal requirements for operating a website in the UK.

### Advanced — Full Compliance
- Everything in Basic, plus:
- Cookie consent management platform
- Accessibility statement
- GDPR data request handling process
- Compliance audit and recommendations

**Benefit:** Full confidence that your site meets GDPR, PECR, and accessibility regulations. Reduces legal risk.

### Multi-Language
- Full site translation into additional languages
- Language switcher

## 6. Monthly Retainer & Support

### Standard Retainer (included with all sites)
- 2 mini projects per month (estimated at no more than 30 minutes each)
- Use them or lose them — they do not roll over
- Examples: text updates, image swaps, adding a new section, tweaking layouts
- Email support for questions and requests

**Benefit:** Your site stays current without the hassle of separate quotes for every small change. Quick turnaround on the things that matter.

### Premium Retainer
- Everything in Standard, plus:
- Monthly report covering uptime, visitor stats, and leads generated
- Performance and SEO health snapshot
- Actionable recommendations based on your data
- Priority response time

**Benefit:** You get full visibility into how your site is performing, what's driving enquiries, and where to improve — backed by data, not guesswork.
